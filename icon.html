Key Club International Convention

The Key Club International Convention (ICON) is THE LARGEST Convention of the year! Taking place June 24-28, 2015 in downtown Indianapolis, this year's ICON is even more special, celebrating the 100th anniversary of Kiwanis International and the 90th anniversary of Key Club International. Not only will you have the chance to meet Key Clubbers from around the world, but Kiwanians, Circl-Kers, and K-Family members of all ages will be gathering at the headquarters of Kiwanis International for a once-in-a-lifetime celebration of service and leadership!

With 1,600 Key Clubbers from all across the United States, Canada and the Caribbean, incredible speakers, and workshops by prominent leaders in the organization, there's something for everyone! Get ready to compete in art and performance competitions, exchange ideas at the Community Impact Fair, represent your District in the International Board elections, watch performances by the internationall acclaimed Hellogoodbye and A Great Big Pile of Leaves, attend workshops ranging from website building to blanket making, and so much more!

The deadline for ICON registration HAS PASSED. However, you can still watch the recap of ICON 2015 on the Key Club International Youtube Channel.

Contests

If you won an award at LTC, you might be eligible to compete at ICON! Check out the Key Club Guidebook for additional details on the rules and regulations. In addition, look over the Contest Submission Guide if you have any questions about eligibility.

Check out the Key Club website for general information on award and for past winners.

Schedule

Unlike previous years, the New York District tour will not be taking place for the 2015 International Convention. However, do not despair! There's a ton of even more exciting adventures waiting in downtown Indianapolis - from visiting Key Club HQ, eating pancakes at the only international all-you-can-eat pancake lunch in the world, partying on Georgia Street (RESERVED just for Key Club International), and vising/shopping/hiking your way around Indiana! Check out more activities on the Key Club agenda!
