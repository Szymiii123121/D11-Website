Who we are?

The Divisional Board is responsible for carrying out the business of Division 11. The board is composed of the Division 11 Lieutenant Governor, the Executive Assistant, the Public Relations Committee Chair, the Divisional Historian, and the Divisional Webmaster.

The Board annually meets to plan Divisional events, fundraisers, and community initiatives.

The District Board’s duties include, but are not limited to:

  • Running workshops and general sessions at the Divisional Training Conference and Fall Rally
  • Planning for DTC (theme, schedule, etc.)
  • Deciding upon the Divisional Projects
  • Serve on Divisional PR and Events Committees
  • Represent the Division at the District and International Levels
  • Assist any and all members of Key Club

Our Progress

Each year, Division 11 chooses goals for the service year. Here is the current progress on the following goals:

  • Fundraising: $15,000
  • The Eliminate Project: $25,000
  • Dues-Paid Members: 13,000
  • Newly Chartered Clubs: 20
