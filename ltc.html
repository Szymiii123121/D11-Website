New York District Leadership Training Conference (LTC)

Each year, the New York District Key Club gets together to celebrate the year of service, elect the new district executive board, meet other Key Clubbers from across the Mighty New York State, and learn about new service opportunities!

Stay tuned for the "Call to Convention" packet for more information on the workshops, awards, elections, performances, and other activities planned for you!

The LTC theme for the 2015-2016 Service Year is Red Carpet - Stars of Service, and the dates are March 18-20, 2016!

Check out the photo gallery for pictures from last year's LTC, taking place at the Desmond Hotel in Albany, NY. The Conference featured speakers such as Evan Wesley from the Thirst Project, representatives from Rustic Pathways, and much more! In addition, the new District Executive Board was elected, incoming LTGs installed, and the outgoing Board retired. The Penny Wars was also won by Divisions 9/10/11, with all proceeds going towards the Eliminate Project!

A huge thanks to all photographers who helped capture the memories from the Conference!
